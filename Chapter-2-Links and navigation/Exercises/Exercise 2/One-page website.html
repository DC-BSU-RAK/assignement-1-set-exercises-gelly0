Inside Out 2

Welcome to Inside Out 2! Explore the latest info, cast, and reviews of the movie!

Plot Overview

Inside Out 2 continues Riley’s story, now a teenager, navigating complex new emotions.

Key Information

  • Release Date: June 14, 2024
  • Studio: Pixar Animation Studios
  • Rating: PG
  • Genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy

Cast & Creative Team

  • Director: Kelsey Mann
  • Joy: Amy Poehler
  • Sadness: Phyllis Smith
  • Disgust: Liza Lapira
  • Fear: Tony Hale
  • Anger: Lewis Black
  • New Emotion (Anxiety): Maya Hawke

Characters

  • Joy: The bubbly leader who finds happiness in everything.
  • Sadness: Sensitive and empathetic, always there when Riley needs reflection.
  • Disgust: Keeps Riley away from bad ideas and worse fashion.
  • Fear: Keeps Riley safe with endless worrying.
  • Anger: Expresses what's unfair with fiery passion.
  • Anxiety: A new addition—an intense planner, sometimes a little too much.
