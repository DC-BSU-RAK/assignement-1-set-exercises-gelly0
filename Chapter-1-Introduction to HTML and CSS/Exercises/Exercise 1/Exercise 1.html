Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

A delicious homemade chocolate chip cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups Nuts

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add vanilla extract and eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Allow cookies to cool before serving.
